Details on the data we retain when you pay us

Our only payment provider so far is Stripe, so for full privacy info, you'll need to read their privacy policy.

When we log into TallyLab's Stripe account, here's what we see in the entry for your payment:

Customer ID An ID generated by Stripe to identify you
Payment Cycle One-time, monthly, or yearly
Payment Amount In US dollars
Date of Last Payment So we know if you're up to date
Encrypted Metadata This is super nerdy, but the way we match a user to their payment info is by testing if they can decrypt the random metadata we saved with their payment info.

That's right, there is no personally identifiable info that we save with your payment info. We can't speak to what Stripe saves, but now you know what we save.
